```

                  (Soul Sand)

                      |

                      |
     🏛️ WEST ◀️——————⚔️——————▶️ 🏛️ EAST

                      |
```

**Pro Tip**: Count 5 blocks from center in each direction. Use F3 if you need coordinates!

## Step 3: AWAKEN YOUR WEAPON! 🔥

1. Put your weapon on the **CENTER** totem
2. Put the soul components on the **FOUR** outer totems
3. Light a **FIRE** at the base of the center totem

# ❓ FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

## "What happens if my weapon BREAKS?!"
😢 **Bad news**: If your soul weapon's durability hits zero... the soul is LOST FOREVER.

💡 **Pro tip**: Use **Mending** and **Unbreaking** enchantments to protect your investment! Your soul weapon is PRECIOUS!

## "Can I use this on ANY tool?"
✅ **YES!** Works on:
- Swords, Pickaxes, Axes, Shovels, Hoes
- Bows, Crossbows, Tridents
- Even modded tools (if configured)!

## "Can I rename my soul weapon?"
✅ **YES!** Use the command `/rituals soul rename <new name>`

You get ONE free rename per level. Choose wisely!

## "Do bonuses stack with existing enchantments?"
✅ **YES!** If your sword already has Sharpness III and you get a Sharpness bonus, it becomes Sharpness IV!

## "What's the point of doing 18 rituals?"
🎯 Each ritual:
- Lets your weapon level HIGHER
- Gives 50% chance for FREE enchantments
- Makes your weapon's personality EVOLVE
- Is an investment in YOUR unique legendary item!

## "Is Level 100 worth the grind?"
🔥 **ABSOLUTELY!** A max-level soul weapon is:
- The strongest version of that weapon possible
- Covered in bonus enchantments
- A PERMANENT trophy of your dedication
- Something NO ONE can take from you!

Don't want to trek back to your totem every time? **CRAFT A SCRYING GLASS!**

## Crafting Recipe

```
      [Amethyst]
  [Amethyst][Spyglass][Amethyst]
      [Amethyst]
```

## How To Use

1. Put your **Soul Weapon** in your **OFFHAND** (press F to swap!)
2. Hold the **Scrying Glass** in your **MAINHAND**
3. **Right-click and look through it** (like a normal spyglass)
4. ✨ **SOUL SYNCED!** ✨

The glass has **5 uses** before it shatters! Use wisely!
